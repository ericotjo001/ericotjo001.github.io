# Agentic AI fantasy to reality: from AI 3.0 to AI 27.5

> **Summary:** A satirical yet analytical deep dive into the future of the agentic web, exploring scenarios from "Agentic Wars" to corporate-dominated ecosystems, and identifying the tech-app stack winners.

## Future Scenarios of the Agentic Internet
* **The Agentic War:** Sovereign AI systems and defensive structures dominate cross-border traffic.
* **The Fragmented World:** The rise of "Agentic Record Labels" (ARL) where startups become "talents" on royalty contracts.
* **Corporate Dominance:** Big Tech maintains the status quo by acquiring breakout players like OpenClaw or Manus.

## The Tech-App Stack View
The "AgentFace" (a consumer-friendly interface like a browser) and the "Grand Agentic Layer" (connecting developers to MCP servers) are the missing links for mass adoption. Edge computation and local powerful models (e.g., Gemma 4) will eventually reduce reliance on paid AI "electricity bills."

## Top Model Investment Picks
Leading AI models suggest focusing on infrastructure (Composio, MintMCP), autonomous labor (Artisan AI, Cognition), and agentic security (Qevlar AI, Armadin).
